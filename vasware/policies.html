Attendance & Punctuality

expand_more

Employees must clock in within 5 minutes of their shift start time. Late arrivals exceeding 10 minutes require supervisor approval. Shift swaps must be submitted 24 hours in advance via the "Time Card" tab.

Safety Protocols

expand_more

High-visibility vests and steel-toed boots are mandatory in all warehouse zones (Zones A-F). Report any spills or hazards immediately to the floor manager. Failure to wear PPE will result in a warning.

coffee

Breaks & Overtime

expand_more

Standard shifts include two 15-minute paid breaks and one 30-minute unpaid lunch. Overtime must be pre-authorized. Double time applies on national holidays.

Code of Conduct

expand_more

Respectful communication is required at all times. Zero tolerance policy for harassment or discrimination. Use of personal mobile devices is restricted to break areas only.
