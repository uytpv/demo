Warehouse Safety

Adherence to these safety protocols is mandatory for all personnel on the warehouse floor. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.

engineering

Personal Protective Equipment

check_circle

High-visibility vests must be worn at all times in operational areas.

check_circle

Steel-toed safety boots are required beyond the yellow line.

check_circle

Use ear protection in zones marked with noise levels above 85dB.

precision_manufacturing

Machinery & Forklifts

warning

Only certified personnel are authorized to operate forklifts.

remove_circle_outline

Never walk under raised loads or machinery arms.

remove_circle_outline

Do not use mobile phones while operating equipment.

medical_services

Emergency Procedures

  • In case of fire, evacuate immediately via the nearest green exit sign.
  • Assembly Point: North Parking Lot, Zone B.
  • Report all injuries, no matter how minor, to the shift supervisor immediately.
  • First Aid kits are located at Columns A4, C8, and the Break Room.
